Events at Fieldstone Kitchen.

Music

Live Jazz Sunday

Every Sunday, 10:00am – 1:00pm

A rotating duo of local jazz musicians plays through the full weekend brunch service — a little something to make Sunday morning feel like what it should. No cover charge, no ticket required. Just show up, find a table, and let the music do its thing.

Seasonal Menu

Holiday Brunch Menu — Christmas Edition

December 21 – 26, 2024

A limited-run holiday menu featuring seasonal specials alongside our classics — think warm spiced porridges, smoked salmon with all the trimmings, and a pastry board that earns its place on the table. Running for six days only, so reservation is strongly recommended.

Community

Community Morning — First Saturday

First Saturday of each month, 8:00am – 10:00am

A monthly informal morning for neighbours, regulars, and anyone curious enough to walk in. Coffee on us for the first hour, light snacks on the table, and good conversation with whoever shows up. No agenda — just a chance to connect with the people who make this neighbourhood worth living in.

Come Say Hello
Seasonal Menu

Thanksgiving Weekend Brunch

October 12 – 14, 2024

Extended Thanksgiving weekend service with a special harvest menu built around the best of the fall season — roasted pumpkin, squash, apple, and all the warming spices that belong on a long weekend table. Our classics are still on offer, but you'll want to see what's new.

Reserve for the Weekend
Special Event

Kitchen Table — Chef's Demo Evening

November 8, 2024

A limited-seat evening where our head chef walks through a four-course seasonal tasting menu — live, in the kitchen, with wine pairing suggestions for each dish. It's the kind of evening that turns into a two-hour conversation about food. Tickets required; capacity is intentionally small.

Spring Menu Launch

April 5, 2025

A celebration of the new season — our full spring menu rolls out with weekend specials, new drinks on the board, and a few surprises worth showing up for. Light, bright, and built around whatever looks best at the market that week. Walk-ins welcome all weekend.
