Why Ridgecrest

Experience You Can See. Standards You Can Trust.

Since 1996, Ridgecrest Construction has been one of the GTA's most dependable general contractors. We're not a franchise, and we don't subcontract our core work to the lowest bidder. Every project has a dedicated site supervisor, and our principals are reachable from first estimate to final sign-off.

  • Over 28 years of continuous operation in the Greater Toronto Area
  • Fully licensed by the Ontario College of Trades and fully insured to $5M liability
  • Deep knowledge of local zoning, permitting, and building code requirements across Peel, Halton, and Toronto
  • Two-year craftsmanship warranty on all completed builds and renovations
