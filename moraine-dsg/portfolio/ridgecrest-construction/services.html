What We Build

Our Services

Ridgecrest Construction handles every phase of the build — from permits and planning through to final finishes. Here's what we do best.

Service 01

Custom Home Builds

Building a custom home is one of the most significant decisions you'll make, and it deserves a contractor who's done it hundreds of times across the GTA. Ridgecrest manages the entire process — lot evaluation, permit application, foundation and framing, mechanical rough-ins, insulation, drywall, millwork, and final inspection.

We work closely with your architect or can recommend architects and designers we've built trusted relationships with over the years. Our dedicated site supervisors are on-site daily and our project managers provide weekly written updates so you always know where your build stands.

  • Full project management from permit to certificate of occupancy
  • Established relationships with structural engineers, HVAC trades, and electrical contractors
  • Transparent pricing with detailed fixed-cost contracts — no surprise invoices
  • Two-year craftsmanship warranty covering all completed work

Service 02

Commercial Renovations

Commercial renovation projects demand a contractor who understands business timelines, health and safety compliance, and the complexity of working in occupied or partially occupied spaces. Ridgecrest has completed commercial renos for office tenants, retail chains, restaurant groups, medical clinics, and light industrial operators across the GTA.

We develop phased construction schedules that minimize operational disruption, coordinate directly with your building management team, and ensure all work meets Ontario Building Code requirements and TSSA standards where applicable.

  • Phased scheduling to minimize business disruption during construction
  • Experience with retail, office, medical, restaurant, and industrial sectors
  • Full compliance with OBC, fire code, and accessibility standards
  • WSIB and comprehensive general liability insurance — certificates on request

Service 03

Home Additions & Extensions

Moving isn't always the answer. A well-executed home addition can give your family the space it needs while preserving the neighbourhood you love — and the equity you've built. Ridgecrest specializes in second-storey additions, rear and side extensions, sunroom additions, and attached or detached garage builds throughout Mississauga, Etobicoke, and the surrounding areas.

We handle zoning verification and variance applications if needed, coordinate with your municipality's building department, and ensure the addition integrates seamlessly with your existing structure — matching exterior finishes, roofline, and materials so the result looks like it was always part of the house.

  • Second-storey additions, rear extensions, and side additions
  • Zoning review and building permit applications handled by our team
  • Exterior material matching — brick, siding, roofing, windows
  • Structural engineer stamp included in all structural addition projects
Plan Your Addition
Service 04

Basement & Foundation Work

A finished basement can add significant living space and resale value to your home. But basement work done poorly — improper moisture control, insufficient headroom, code-non-compliant egress windows — creates headaches that cost far more to fix later. Ridgecrest takes foundation and basement work seriously from day one.

We offer underpinning and bench-pinning to add headroom in low basements, exterior and interior waterproofing, full basement finishing including secondary suite creation for legal rental income, and structural crack repair. All basement work is permitted and inspected to current Ontario Building Code standards.

  • Underpinning and bench-pinning for increased ceiling height
  • Interior and exterior waterproofing with sump pump installation
  • Legal secondary suite creation with full permits
  • Structural crack repair and foundation stabilization

How We Work

Our Process

Every Ridgecrest project follows the same four-stage process — designed to keep you informed, protected, and confident at every step.

1

Consultation

We meet on-site to review your goals, evaluate the space, discuss budget expectations, and answer your questions — no obligation, no pressure.

2

Design & Planning

We develop a detailed scope of work, coordinate drawings and engineering, submit permit applications, and provide a fixed-cost contract for your approval.

3

Construction

Our crew gets to work with a dedicated site supervisor managing daily progress. You receive weekly written updates and have direct access to your project manager at all times.

4

Final Walkthrough

We walk the completed project with you, address any deficiencies on the spot, obtain your final inspection sign-off, and hand over your warranty documentation.
