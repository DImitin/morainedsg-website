YOUR FIRST VISIT

Getting Started is Simple.

Whether you're coming in for a new injury, managing a long-standing condition, or simply want to feel better day-to-day, here's what to expect.

1

Book Online or Call

Use our online booking system to choose your practitioner, discipline, and a time that works for you. Same-week appointments are often available.

2

Complete Your Intake

A short health history intake is sent to you ahead of your appointment so your practitioner can arrive prepared. No paper forms in the waiting room.

3

Assessment & Treatment

Your first session combines a thorough assessment with hands-on treatment. Your practitioner will explain their findings and outline a realistic plan.

4

Your Ongoing Plan

Leave with a clear home-exercise plan, a follow-up schedule, and direct billing handled so your insurance coverage is used with zero hassle.

Common Questions.

No referral is required to book with any of our practitioners. Physiotherapy, RMT, chiropractic, and acupuncture are all self-referral services in Ontario. That said, some extended health insurance plans may require a referral note for reimbursement purposes — it's worth checking your policy before your first visit, and our team is happy to assist if you have questions.

Yes. We direct bill to most major extended health providers including Manulife, Sun Life, Canada Life, Desjardins, Blue Cross, and Green Shield. At your first visit, simply bring your insurance card and we will submit the claim on your behalf. Any remaining balance is collected at the time of service. We encourage you to check your annual coverage limits in advance.

A good starting point: if your concern is a movement limitation, injury, or post-surgical recovery, physiotherapy is usually the best first step. For muscle tightness, stress, or tension-related pain, registered massage therapy is highly effective. Spine, joint, and disc issues respond well to chiropractic care. And for chronic or systemic concerns — pain, stress, sleep, or hormonal health — acupuncture may be the right fit. If you're unsure, call us and we'll help you choose.
