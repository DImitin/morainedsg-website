Our History

How We Got Here

Every milestone below is a reflection of the trust Brampton homeowners have placed in us. We don't take that lightly.

2004

Founded in Brampton

Started as a one-truck operation by [placeholder name], a Red Seal journeyman plumber with a decade of experience and a belief in straightforward, honest work.

2008

First Commercial Contract

Secured our first multi-unit residential contract, servicing a property management company in central Brampton. Grew to a two-truck team.

2010

Expanded to Mississauga & Caledon

Word of mouth brought us into new service areas. Added a third licensed technician and began offering 24/7 emergency coverage full-time.

2015

Added Water Heater & Waterproofing Services

Responded to customer demand by expanding our scope — bringing in specialized training for tankless water heater installation and basement drainage systems.

2020

5,000th Service Call

A milestone that reminded us why we do this work. Four trucks, a full team of licensed technicians, and the same approach we started with: show up, do the job right, charge a fair price.

Today

Serving All of Peel Region

Now covering Brampton, Mississauga, Caledon, Georgetown, Bolton, and Orangeville. Still locally owned. Still your neighbours.
