Services & Pricing

Transparent, upfront pricing for every job. A written quote before we start — no surprises.

Residential Plumbing

Kitchen, bathroom, laundry, and floor drains. Camera inspection available on request. Hydro-jetting for severe blockages. Includes assessment of drain condition and cause of blockage.

Supply and install. We carry major brands and can install customer-supplied fixtures. Includes shutoff valve inspection and supply line replacement if needed.

Running, leaking, rocking, or just time for an upgrade. We diagnose and fix internal components or supply and install a full replacement. Old toilet removal included with replacement.

Copper, PEX, and ABS piping. Leak detection included with every repair. We locate the source before opening walls — minimizing damage and repair cost.

Full repiping for older homes with galvanized, lead, or deteriorating copper. We assess, quote, and repipe with modern materials. Scope and pricing provided after on-site walk-through.

Water Heaters

Tank and tankless units. We diagnose thermostat, element, igniter, and valve issues. Most repairs completed in a single visit. Parts carried on the truck.

Includes removal and disposal of old unit, supply of new unit, and full installation. We help you choose the right capacity and efficiency rating for your household.

Energy-efficient upgrade. Endless hot water, lower operating costs. Includes gas line or electrical upgrade assessment, venting, and installation. Wall-mounted units save significant space.

Basement & Drainage

Primary and battery backup sump pump options available. Keeps your basement dry during spring thaw and heavy rain. Includes pit assessment, pump selection, and discharge line routing.

Prevent sewer backup from entering your home during heavy rain events. This work may qualify for the City of Brampton's residential basement flooding protection subsidy. Ask us for details.

Interior and exterior solutions including weeping tile systems, membrane application, and window well drains. Pricing quoted after on-site assessment of foundation and drainage conditions.

Emergency Service

Flat dispatch fee for after-hours emergency calls. This fee is applied directly to the cost of the repair if you proceed with the work — you're not just paying for a visit. We diagnose, quote, and get started.

A 25% surcharge is added to standard labour rates for work performed on evenings (after 6pm), weekends, and statutory holidays. This does not apply during regular business hours.

All prices are estimates. Final pricing is provided in a written quote before any work begins. HST is extra. Prices subject to change based on site conditions.
