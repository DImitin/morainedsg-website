About Moraine DSG

A small studio.
A clear point of view.

We started Moraine because small businesses in the GTA deserve the same quality digital work as anyone else — without the agency markup or the runaround.

Our Story

Where the name comes from

A moraine is the material left behind by a glacier — the result of slow, persistent work over time. It's not dramatic. It doesn't announce itself. But it shapes the landscape for good. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

Moraine DSG was started by Sam Moncada and Nick Sein — two people who saw a real gap in the market and decided to do something about it.

Small businesses in the GTA run on tight margins. Every dollar is accounted for. And yet a well-built website — something that actually brings in customers, ranks locally, and represents the business properly — can be the difference between getting found and getting passed over. The problem isn't that business owners don't see the value. It's that the options have always been too expensive, too complicated, or too hands-off.

We built Moraine DSG to close that gap. Professional websites and digital services, built for small businesses, priced so they're actually accessible, and managed by the same people who built them.

Every project we take on is handled personally. You'll know who's working on your site. You'll get straight answers about what something costs and why. No hidden fees, no junior handoffs, no waiting three weeks to hear back.

We're based in the GTA and we work exclusively with small businesses.

How We Work

Three things we don't compromise on

Craftsmanship

We build things properly. That means clean code, fast load times, and a design that still holds up after the first coat of paint fades. We don't cut corners to hit a faster turnaround.

If something isn't right, we fix it. That's not a policy — it's just how we do the job.

Transparency

Every quote we send is itemized. Every timeline we give is real. If something changes mid-project, we tell you immediately — before it affects your bill.

We don't hide fees in the fine print or use confusing language to obscure what you're actually paying for.

Partnership

We're not a vendor. We're not a ticket queue. We're the people you call when something's broken at 7am the day of a big promotion, and we'll actually pick up.

Our clients stay with us because working together gets easier over time, not harder.
